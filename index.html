What is MORGANSSapplyValues?

MORGANSSapplyValues is a political compass test that combines the best questions of the original SapplyValues with the bst questions of Shodan's version + a few of my own. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a political compass.

* A few questions have been reworded as I feel some of them were poorly written.

There are questions in the test.

Why use this version?

Both the original and the Shodan version have a number of biased questions. The Shodan version words things better, but has a bit of a libertarian bias. Here, I take the best of both tests and combine them.

Your questions / results suck!

You will have to complain to the original Sapply creator. I did not make this test, I simply changed the UI.
